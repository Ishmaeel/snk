/ news : snk


May, 2000

Fix goes to Hollywood ! - Our label, Hammer Müzik, has licenced "Fix" to Pavement Records, one of the leading American labels. For starters, 650 of our digipacks are going to be distributed by Pavement.

March, 2000

A new project - SNK Guitarist Burak has started an auxiliary project, "DreamLands", which has a swedish-style, melodic death-metal sound. The idea was of a one-man project, for about a year. However, DreamLands has recently evolved into a band project with new members. The band has started studio work and will soon be recording their debut.

November, 1999

He's in the army now. - Taner has gone to carry out his military service. He'll be back in eight months. (Carry on, soldier!)

September, 1999

Hakan has left the band - As a result of disagreements on the band's musical progression, drummer Hakan is no longer a member of SNK. We wish him good luck.

August, 1999

A bitter separation - Bass-player Ozan has gone to the United States for educational purposes for two years. SNK will proceed on course without him for a while. (We miss you, big man!)

April, 1999

Album is out! - Sterile Noiz Klan's debut album, "Fix", has been released under Hammer Müzik label. Album contains industrial and hard-core elements with some electronic and ambient touches. Look for it in your favorite music market!

January, 1999

Fresh blood! - SNK has grown musically stronger with the addition of Taner to the band. He will be responsible for the electronic elements of SNK sound.

July, 1998

In the studio - SNK has completed the recording of "fix", in Studio Forte, Ýstanbul. The recording took 120 hours of studio work.
